### Biography
Çalgan Aygün is a DevOps Lead at Finalcheck, specializing in providing solutions for individual verification. Previously, he worked as a backend and cloud engineer at QuarPay, focusing on modernizing infrastructure and developing faster payment systems. With a background as a software developer at My Security Analytics, his experience extends to analytics software for business, fraud, and government security. 

Çalgan is recognized as one of the Google Cloud Innovators Champions on Serverless App Development, and is actively involved as an organizer and speaker for GDG Ankara and GDG Cloud Ankara. He has expertise in serverless cloud services, backend development, OSINT, document AI services, face recognition, amateur radio, payment systems, and high-scale scraping. Additionally, he has a history as a freelance DevOps/Developer in various fields.
